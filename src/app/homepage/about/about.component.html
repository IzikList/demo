About us
We believe in the power of technology to remove barriers and drive transparency and equality to the Life settlements market.

Our goal is to simplify and improve the way policyholders and investors approach this asset.
We built and designed a two-sided-marketplace, were both sides benefits.
For investor
We provide the accredited investor a easy to use one-button-perches platform, enabling him to invest directly & seamlessly into many policies at once, we use algorithms to calculate and ensure diversification, quality of policies & IRR forecasts. Through the platform, the investor becomes a true portal owner in a pure alpha assets.
Get started
For policyholder
We provide the policyholder with the opportunity to continue holding on their valuable life insurance policy while at the same time relieve them from the ongoing premiums, we do this by turning the policy into a liquid asset and through the platform, we enable small investors to finance the upcoming premiums for a stake in the asset
